Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PureStream. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our application. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices when you use PureStream, a lightweight application that searches YouTube videos for audio streams and plays them back for you.

Key Point: PureStream does not require any personal information to use the application. You can enjoy the full functionality of PureStream without creating an account or providing any personal data.

2. What Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

PureStream does not require you to provide any personal information such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Account credentials
  • Payment information
  • Location data

The application is fully functional without any registration or personal data submission.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use PureStream, we may automatically collect limited technical information to help us understand how the application performs:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version you are using
  • Usage Data: Information about which features you use and how frequently you use them
  • Search Queries: The audio streams you search for (not stored on our servers, processed locally)
  • Performance Metrics: App crash reports and performance issues (to help us improve stability)

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2.3 YouTube Integration

PureStream searches YouTube for audio streams. When you search:

  • Your search queries are sent to YouTube's search API
  • YouTube's privacy policies apply to their data collection
  • We do not store your search history on our servers
  • Search data is processed locally on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited technical information we collect for the following purposes only:

  • To provide and maintain the PureStream application
  • To improve app performance and stability
  • To fix bugs and technical issues
  • To understand general usage patterns (anonymously)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any other commercial purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties, except:

4.1 YouTube

Search queries are sent to YouTube as part of the application's core functionality. Please refer to YouTube's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose anonymized information if required by law, such as in response to valid legal requests from authorities.

4.3 No Other Sharing

We do not share your information with analytics companies, advertising networks, or other service providers.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. PureStream implements the following security measures:

  • All communication with YouTube is encrypted (HTTPS)
  • No personal information is stored on our servers
  • Search history and playback data remain on your device only
  • Regular security reviews and updates

Since PureStream does not collect personal information, the risk of data breaches affecting your personal data is minimal.

6. Data Retention

PureStream does not retain personal information. Any technical data we collect is:

  • Retained only for as long as necessary to improve the application
  • Deleted or anonymized after a reasonable period
  • Never used to create a personal profile of you

7. Your Privacy Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is nothing for you to access, correct, or delete. However, you have full control over your device:

  • You can uninstall the application at any time
  • You can control app permissions through your device settings
  • Your local search history and playback data remains under your control
  • You can clear your device's app cache and data through system settings

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

PureStream does not use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies. We do not track your behavior across the internet or link your usage to any personal identifier.

9. Third-Party Services

PureStream integrates with YouTube to provide audio stream search and playback functionality. We are not responsible for YouTube's privacy practices. We encourage you to review YouTube's Privacy Policy.

10. Children's Privacy

PureStream is not specifically designed for children. However, since the application does not collect any personal information and requires no registration, children can use it safely. Parents should be aware that the app accesses YouTube content, which may have parental control considerations.

11. International Usage

PureStream can be used from any location. Since we do not collect personal information, data location and international data transfer concerns do not apply to PureStream.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page when we make material changes. Your continued use of PureStream following any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights. Since PureStream does not collect personal information, CCPA rights do not substantially apply to our application. You have the right to know that we do not:

  • Collect personal information about you
  • Sell or share your personal information
  • Create profiles or maintain databases about you

14. European Union Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides certain rights regarding personal data. Since PureStream does not collect personal information, GDPR protections regarding personal data do not substantially apply. We are committed to:

  • Transparent data practices
  • Minimal data collection
  • User privacy by default
